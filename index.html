About Wilken

Wilken is an e-commerce company that offers a wide range of smart technologies such as smartwatches and earphones based on your personal experiences, lifestyle needs and the dynamic technological new necessities of high-end quality. The company is working on expanding its works in different domains within the technology industry as well as other industries, in order to provide solutions to reach everyone’s needs, providing Luxury systems of Technology products to customers worldwide.

Overview

During the past few years, there has been a rapid development of sensor and systematic technologies, which enables new types of products. Our products offer a set of unique properties like being context-aware, adaptive, and proactive and the ability to support the lifecycle. We introduce new ways to relate to users, as well as new sets of challenges and opportunities. We create intelligent products, futuristic, simple to use as well as efficient, and accessible to all customers. Such as smartwatches, advanced earphones and we are in progress to offer a new experience of products with unique industrial designs. The company currently works within the technological domain selling smartwatches and earphones of top tier quality; however, their mission is to expand their brand and branch out within other industries like fashion wear, furniture, etc.
